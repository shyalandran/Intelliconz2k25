Role Camera Action

Short

Welcome to Roll Camera Action, your ultimate platform for all things filmmaking! This event is a golden opportunity for aspiring and professional filmmakers to showcase their storytelling skills, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and bring their cinematic vision to life. Creative Collaboration: Work with fellow filmmakers, writers, actors, and editors to craft a compelling short film.

Participants are required to prepare both a complete short film and a one-minute teaser trailer. In Round 1, submit the teaser showcasing your storytelling and creativity; selected entries move to Round 2, where the full film must be submitted as per the guidelines.
Note:-
To maintain a respectful and inclusive environment, all films must adhere to the following content guidelines:
✅ No adult (18+) content.
✅ No offensive, violent, or disturbing themes.
✅ No explicit language or hate speech.
✅ Films should be original and align with the given theme.

Round 1: Online Prelims – "Cinematic Pitch & Teaser Challenge"

Task: Participants must submit a 1-minute teaser/trailer of their short film.

Purpose: Tests their ability to create suspense, attract an audience, and deliver a strong message in a short time. Judges evaluate their storytelling skills before watching the full film

Timeframe: Submission Deadline: (Day 3) .



Round 2: Offline Finals – "Live Screening & Filmmakers' Battle" "

Segment 1: Short Film Screening & Q&A The top 10-15 films are screened in front of the audience & judges.

Each team gets 5 minutes to present their filmmaking process, inspirations, and challenges.

The audience can ask one or two questions (engagement factor).

Segment 2: Filmmaker’s Creative Twist Challenge (Bonus Round for Extra Points!) Each team is given a random filmmaking challenge (e.g., "Recreate a scene from your film using only props on stage," "Describe your film in one line, one word, and one emoji").

Judges score this bonus round for extra engagement.
