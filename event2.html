Code Bonanza

Coding Contest

Join us for an exciting and intellectually stimulating Coding Event at Panimalar Engineering College's Technical Symposium! Unleash your coding skills and creativity as you take on thrilling challenges, compete with passionate programmers, and push the boundaries of innovation. This is your chance to showcase your problem-solving abilities, think outside the box, and rise to the occasion in an electrifying competition. Don't miss this incredible opportunity to test your expertise, connect with like-minded coding enthusiasts, and make unforgettable memories. Gear up, code your way to victory, and let innovation lead the way!

Round 1: The Coding Lottery Challenge!

To write a program in a specified language that matches given outputs

⁠Pick Your Lots: One team member draws 2 output lots and 1 programming language(C, Python and Java) lot to determine your challenge.

Code Within 30 Minutes: ⁠Use the lots to write the program and match the output within 30 minutes.

⁠No External Help: Work on your own, Googling or outside assistance is not allowed.

Advance to Round 3:The selected teams with the correct program will move on to the 3rd round!





Round 2: Program Hunt Challenge !

Find the exact program that generates the given desired output.

Pick Slip: Each team picks a slip with a desired output.

Search Folders: Explore provided folders to find the program matching the output.

No external helps are allowed.

⁠Time limit: 30 minutes.
